Secret Place

Secret Place is a web application created to help people grow spiritually, stay connected with others, and build consistent habits in their daily walk with God.

It’s a simple and welcoming space where users can read, reflect, share, and support one another.

What is this project about?

The idea behind Secret Place is to bring together a few important aspects of Christian life in one place:

Personal Bible reading
Group interaction
Daily devotionals
Prayer support
Meaningful discussions

Instead of using multiple tools, this platform offers everything in one organised and easy-to-use system.

What can you do here?
-Track your Bible reading

-You can save where you stopped reading and come back anytime.
The system also keeps a history of your progress, so you can see your journey over time.

-Read and create devotionals
Leaders can share devotionals with their groups
Admins can share devotionals with everyone

You can also write personal notes while reading.

-Join groups

Connect with others in smaller groups where you can:

Share devotionals
Participate in discussions
Attend group events
-Participate in discussions

Start or join conversations about faith, life, or anything meaningful.
Moderation ensures a respectful and safe environment.

-Share prayer requests

Post prayer requests and support others.
Requests are moderated to keep the space safe and appropriate.

-Create and attend gatherings

Leaders and admins can create events:

For everyone (global)
Or for specific groups
-Different roles

The platform has three types of users:

Members – use the platform and participate
Leaders – manage groups and create content
Admins – oversee the whole system
- What makes this project special?
Simple and clean design
Focused on community and personal growth
Role-based features (different experience depending on the user)
Real-world structure (similar to modern web platforms)
- What to expect

This is a fully working application, but also part of a learning project.
You can expect:

A functional and stable system
Core features working end-to-end
Continuous improvements in the future
- Final message

This project was created with the intention of encouraging people to spend more time with God and build stronger connections with others.
